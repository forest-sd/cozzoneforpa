About Cozzone for PA

Christina Cozzone is an accomplished public servant and community advocate with a rich background in Pennsylvania politics. With over a decade of experience in local government, she has served on the Chester County Board of Commissioners, championing initiatives to enhance the quality of life for residents.

Background

Cozzone's commitment to community activism is reflected in her active engagement with local organizations, ensuring that the voices of constituents are heard and represented. Her experience spans public health, environmental protection, economic development, and education policy.

Vision for Pennsylvania

Cozzone for PA represents a vision of inclusive, responsive governance that prioritizes the needs of all Pennsylvanians. Key priorities include expanding access to healthcare, protecting natural resources, supporting small businesses, and investing in public education.

Get Involved

Democracy works best when citizens are engaged. Whether through volunteering, attending town halls, or simply staying informed, every voice matters in shaping Pennsylvania's future.
